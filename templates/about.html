Know More About Us ?





Our Goal
Our prime goal is to create a platform for hearing/speech impaired people, which they can use to communicate smoothly at any circumstance.





Practice Your Skills
When the user communicates with an oppositite party using sign language, the pre-trained model then uses the recorded gestures to relay the desired message in the form of text or speech to the receiver.

The Model currently supports 26 alphabets signs in ASL , It can be used by beginner user to practice his skills against pre-trained model.
